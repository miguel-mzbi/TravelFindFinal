Organization Description

We are a international organization with the objective of helping families to locate and eventually reunite with their lost families. Everyday we are increasing our number of affiliated hospitals and medical service location, police and investigations services all around the world. When this services locate or attend the missing person they provide a basic description with all the possible information, so a person serching for their relative can get in touch with us, and together get trough the identification, validation and contact process.
